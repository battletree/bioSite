The subject of this website will be Eugene. D. Kwon. Eugene is a doctor at mayo Clinic in Rochester Minnesota where he treats cancer patients from all over the world. Throughout his life it seems that he has gone from one expectation to another but always with a very realistic expectation of the unpredictable nature of life. Quick to admit his own faults, Eugene made for an easy interview subject. He was the first to admit that he was late to grow up and difficult to convince of the most obvious truth to even the most recent of timelines.
